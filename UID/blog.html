Our Web Development Journey

We started this project as a simple idea to create an intuitive e-commerce platform for fashion lovers. With time, it evolved into a full-stack responsive application using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Our team worked collaboratively, discussing UI/UX, optimizing images, and ensuring mobile compatibility.

10/04/2025

Challenges and Growth

Like any real-world project, we faced bugs, responsive issues, and backend API design hurdles. But these challenges made us better. We learned how to debug, work with Git, and even integrated a chatbot to improve user engagement. Each problem gave us a chance to grow stronger.

18/04/2025

UI/UX Design Decisions

One of the most exciting parts was designing the interface. We researched top e-commerce brands, analyzed color schemes, and carefully planned product placement. We kept the user at the center, ensuring easy navigation, fast loading, and clear CTAs.

22/04/2025

Integration of KrizBot

We created KrizBot – our custom chatbot assistant to make the shopping experience more interactive. Built with JavaScript, the bot can answer FAQs, guide users through shopping, and offer a touch of personality to the website. It was a fun and innovative addition!

26/04/2025

What We Learned

This project helped us understand real-world workflows, time management, and the importance of testing. From frontend polish to backend logic, we got hands-on experience with the entire development cycle. And we’re just getting started.
